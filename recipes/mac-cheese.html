Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Just macaroni and cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 box of elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Black pepper
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stiffing occasionally untill cooked through. 8 minutes
  2. At the same time melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pur in milk slowly, while stirring continiously. Continue to cook and stir until mixture is smooth and bubbling, about 5 minutes, making sure the milk doesn't burn
  5. Add cheddar cheese and stir until melted, 2 to 4 minutes
  6. Drain Macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until coated
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
